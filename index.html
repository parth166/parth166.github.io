    Technologies Used: Pytorch, Hugging Face, Tensorflow

  • Leveraging Multimodal Bitranformer architecture (extending it to multimodal cross attention) for experimenting with fusion techinques to further the model's accuracy.

    • Scalable Product Recommendation Engine (Data Engineering)
